Sunday

All Sunday activities (except for the Ideation Session) will be held in Spangler Hall at the Harvard Business School (campus map). Spangler Auditorium, where most of the activities will be held, is in the basement of Spangler Hall. The Meredith (Registration) and Williams rooms (Lunch + Plenary) are near the Hall entrance.

8:00-9:00AM Breakfast and Registration Meredith Room, Spangler Hall, Harvard Business School ("HBS")
9:00-9:30AM Morning Keynote Gautam Raghavan Spangler Auditorium, HBS
9:30-10:15AM In the Service, Out of the Closet Kristin Beck, Daniel Lennox-Choate, Emily Miller, Jimmy Pearson Spangler Auditorium, HBS
10:30-11:15AM The LGBTQ Athlete Patrick Burke, Caitlin Cahow, Nevin Caple, Chris Mosier, James Nutter, Jose Estevez Spangler Auditorium, HBS
11:20AM-1:00PM Prism Community Speakers Series II Sa'ed Atshan, James Esseks, Daniel Heller, Ken Mayer Spangler Auditorium, HBS
1:15-2:00PM Lunch Plenary: The Workplace & Employment Kenji Yoshino, Christie Smith Williams Room, Spangler Hall, HBS
2:15-4:00PM Ideation Session (Altitude, Inc.) Alex Tee, Ravi Prasad (Altitude Inc.) Batten Hall Hives, HBS
4:00-5:30PM Closing Keynote & Remarks Ken Mehlman and Frances Frei Spangler Auditorium, HBS
